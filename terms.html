Terms of Service

Last updated: July 2026

What softlisteners is

softlisteners provides paid, one-to-one listening sessions over the phone. A listener gives you their attention for the length of session you've booked. That is the entirety of the service.

What softlisteners is not

softlisteners is not therapy, counselling, coaching, medical care, or crisis intervention. Our listeners are not licensed clinicians, and nothing said during a session should be treated as professional advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are in crisis or in danger, please contact your local emergency services immediately.

Booking and payment

  • Sessions are booked in advance and paid for via UPI before the call takes place.
  • You'll receive an on-screen confirmation and a confirmation email once your booking is complete.
  • Prices are listed on the pricing section of our site and may change from time to time.

Rescheduling and lateness

You can reschedule a session by contacting us ahead of your booked time. If you're late, your listener will wait a short while past the start time; after that, the session may need to be rebooked.

Conduct

Sessions are meant to be respectful spaces for both you and your listener. We reserve the right to end a call or decline future bookings in cases of abuse, threats, or harassment directed at a listener.

Confidentiality

We don't record calls or share what's discussed with anyone outside your session. See our Privacy Policy for details on how your booking information is handled.

Limitation of service

softlisteners is provided as a listening service only. We make no claims about improving mental health, treating any condition, or producing a specific emotional outcome. Use of the service is at your own discretion.

Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Continued use of softlisteners after changes are posted means you accept the updated terms.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to hello@softlisteners.com.
