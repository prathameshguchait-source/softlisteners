Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

When you book a session, we ask for your name (or first name, if you'd prefer to stay more anonymous), email address, phone number, chosen session details, and a UPI transaction ID to confirm payment. If you choose to add notes when booking, we store those too.

How we use it

  • To confirm and schedule your listening session
  • To connect your call at the time you've chosen
  • To send you a confirmation email with your session details
  • To help you look up your own past bookings

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not build profiles about you beyond what's needed to run your sessions.

What we don't do

  • We don't record or transcribe your calls.
  • We don't sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We don't share the content of your conversation with anyone, including other listeners.

How long we keep it

Booking details are retained only as long as needed to provide the service and meet basic accounting requirements. You can request deletion of your booking history at any time by contacting us.

Your choices

You can request to view, update, or delete the information we hold about you by emailing hello@softlisteners.com. You may also choose to share only minimal identifying information (such as a first name) when booking.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@softlisteners.com.
