softlisteners is a one-on-one, confidential listening service. You talk, someone listens — fully, quietly, without an agenda.

Whether you're feeling lonely, overwhelmed, stressed, heartbroken, or you simply need to think out loud, you're welcome here.

This is a listening service, not a substitute for therapy, medical care, or emergency services.

What happens during a session

A phone call. Your voice. Someone else's full attention.

Every session looks a little different, because every person does. But here's roughly what to expect.

at the start

A calm hello

Your listener picks up at the time you booked. No forms to fill out on the call, no small talk you don't want — just an open, unhurried "how are you, really?"

during the call

You lead the conversation

You talk as much or as little as you like, about whatever is on your mind. Your listener stays present and attentive — the pace is entirely yours.

at the end

No follow-up required

When your time is up, the call ends gently. There's no summary, no homework, no pressure to feel a certain way afterward — just space that was yours.

We don't promise a particular outcome or feeling by the end of a call. Some people feel lighter; others just feel heard. Both are enough.

Why softlisteners exists

Not everyone who's struggling needs a solution.

Most of us have had a day where we didn't need advice, a five-step plan, or someone telling us it'll be okay. We just needed to say the thing out loud — to someone who wasn't going to judge it, fix it, or rush us through it.

softlisteners exists for exactly that moment. It isn't therapy, and it isn't a replacement for professional care. It's simply a person, on the phone, giving you their full attention for as long as you've booked.

Sometimes that's all a heavy day needs — someone willing to sit with you in it, quietly, without an agenda of their own.

What makes it different

No advice. No judgment. No diagnosis. No fixing.

Most support spaces want to guide you, treat you, or solve you. We do one thing, and we do it with care: we listen.

no advice

We won't tell you what to do

This isn't coaching. We won't hand you a plan or tell you what we'd do in your shoes — this is your space to think out loud, at your own pace.

no judgment

Nothing you say is judged

Say the thought you haven't said to anyone else — the messy, unfinished, embarrassing one. It's safe to say it here.

no diagnosis

We won't label what you're feeling

We're not clinicians and we won't name a condition or assess your mental health. If that's what you need, we'll always point you toward a licensed professional instead.

no fixing

We won't rush to make it better

We're not here to solve your situation in twenty minutes. We're here to hold space for it — that's often worth more than a quick fix.
